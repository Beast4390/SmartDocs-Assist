SmartDoc Assistant

Offline Enterprise AI Knowledge Intelligence Platform

Enterprise Architectural Principles

SmartDocs Assistant represents a paradigm shift in secure, containerized knowledge management. Designed specifically for environments requiring strict intellectual property protection and regulatory compliance, SmartDocs houses intelligence parameters and files entirely within local servers or air-gapped container boundaries.

Zero Network Leakage

Every float vector computation, speech-to-text transcript, and generative response occurs locally on the hardware.

Linear Scalability

Modular endpoints and lightweight FAISS vector stores enable scaling file catalogs without heavy memory spikes.

Interoperability

Direct support for standard formats like PDFs, DOCX, PPTX presentations, and audio waveforms.
